About

Robomatonix is an EdTech startup, approved by the Government of Rajasthan, dedicated to transforming STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) education through hands-on, interactive learning experiences. Founded by Swetansh Bansal, we inspire and empower students with engaging programs such as 3D pen bootcamps, electronics workshops, etc. Our mission is to foster creativity, critical thinking, and practical skills, preparing students for future success in a technology-driven world.

Our Programs

3D Pen Bootcamp

Empower students to unleash their creativity by mastering the use of 3D pens to create models and designs & apply their skills in creative projects.

Innovative Workshops

Students learn about electricity, identify conductors, and create circuits with LED cards.


Drone Pilot Training

Students learn to control and fly drones, understanding drone construction and functionality.


Robotics Workshop

Students learn about sensors, Arduino, IoT devices, and coding languages. They create projects using sensors and Arduino, integrating their knowledge into practical applications.

Robomatonix Champions League (RCL)

RCL is a school level competition featuring exciting events like Robo-War, Robo-Soccer, Black Line Follower, and Robo-Racer, where students showcase their robotics skills.

Robotics/STEM Lab

We sets up STEM-Robotics labs in schools, equipping students with tools for hands-on learning in science and technology.
